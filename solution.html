LiveMarine Project

In LiveMarine project, there are 10 underwater high resolution cameras that are mounted to floating bases in the Pacific Ocean. The cameras monitor the environment, such as moving fish stocks, animals in that habitat as well as rare passengers. They live stream and record to the research base where all data is analysed simultaneously.

As a part of the LiveMarine project, you can subscribe to our webcam channel by paying a fee on weekly or monthly basis. The subscription gives you free access to the live stream of any of the cameras or recorded sessions including interesting ocean animals and exciting observation with commentary from our marine experts located on the research base. In addition, you can live chat with our experts if you have any questions regarding the videos or live stream.
